<p><strong>The Seismic Simulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uniaxial Seismic Simulators</li><li>Biaxial Seismic Simulators</li><li>6DOF Seismic Simulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seismic simulators market comprises three primary types: uniaxial, biaxial, and 6DOF (six degrees of freedom) simulators. Uniaxial seismic simulators test responses along a single axis, primarily for simple structures. Biaxial simulators assess behavior in two dimensions, suitable for more complex designs. In contrast, 6DOF simulators provide comprehensive testing across three translational and three rotational axes, enabling detailed analysis of dynamic behavior under realistic seismic conditions. Each type serves specific needs in structural engineering and seismic research.</p></p>
