<p><p>The self-adhesive sticker paper market is categorized into three main types: permanent, removable, and re-applicable. Permanent type stickers adhere firmly to surfaces and are designed for long-term use, making them ideal for labels and branding. Removable type allows for easy repositioning without residue, suitable for temporary applications like promotions or seasonal items. Re-applicable stickers can be peeled off and stuck back multiple times, providing flexibility for consumers who want to change designs frequently while maintaining adhesion quality.</p></p>
