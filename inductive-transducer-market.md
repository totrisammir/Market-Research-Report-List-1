<p><p>Inductive transducers convert physical quantities into electrical signals and are categorized into three main types. Electromagnetic transducers utilize electromagnetic principles to sense changes, commonly used in position and motion detection. Piezoelectric transducers generate electrical signals in response to mechanical stress, ideal for vibrations and pressure measurements. Other types encompass various technologies, including capacitive and optical transducers, broadening applications across industries like automotive, healthcare, and aerospace. Each type serves distinct functions based on their sensing principles.</p></p>
