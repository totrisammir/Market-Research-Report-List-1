<p><p>The Modular Isolation Room market encompasses two primary types: Positive Pressure Isolation Rooms and Negative Pressure Isolation Rooms. Positive Pressure Isolation Rooms are designed to protect patients with weakened immune systems by maintaining higher internal pressure, preventing external contaminants from entering. Conversely, Negative Pressure Isolation Rooms are used to contain airborne pathogens, keeping the interior at a lower pressure to prevent the escape of infectious agents to surrounding areas. Both types are essential in healthcare settings for controlling infection and ensuring patient safety.</p></p>
