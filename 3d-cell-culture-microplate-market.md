<p><strong>The 3D Cell Culture Microplate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scaffold-based</li><li>Scaffold-free</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D cell culture microplate market can be categorized into scaffold-based and scaffold-free types. Scaffold-based systems utilize supportive structures made from biomaterials to guide cell organization and mimic the extracellular matrix, promoting tissue-like environments. In contrast, scaffold-free systems rely on cell-cell interactions without external support, allowing cells to form spheroids or organoids naturally. Each type offers unique advantages for drug testing, regenerative medicine, and disease modeling, catering to various research and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
