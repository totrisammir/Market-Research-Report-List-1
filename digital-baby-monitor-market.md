<p><p>The digital baby monitor market can be divided into two main types: interactive and non-interactive monitors. Interactive monitors allow two-way communication, enabling parents to hear and speak to their baby through the device. These often include features like video streaming, night vision, and temperature monitoring. Non-interactive monitors primarily focus on audio transmission, providing sound alerts without additional functionalities like video feeds. While interactive monitors offer more engagement and features, non-interactive models are typically simpler and more cost-effective.</p></p>
