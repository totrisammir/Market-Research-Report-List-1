<p><p>The tea picker market consists of two main types: selective picking machines and non-selective picking machines. Selective picking machines are designed to harvest only the specific leaves needed, ensuring high-quality tea and minimal damage to plants. In contrast, non-selective picking machines gather both leaves and stems indiscriminately, resulting in a faster but potentially lower-quality harvest. Both machine types cater to different needs in the tea production industry, balancing efficiency with quality based on the producer's goals.</p></p>
