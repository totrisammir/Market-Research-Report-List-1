<p><p>The portable and transportable neonatal ventilators market is categorized into invasive and non-invasive ventilators. Invasive ventilators involve intubation, providing mechanical support directly through the lungs, suitable for critically ill neonates requiring advanced respiratory assistance. Non-invasive ventilators, on the other hand, use techniques such as CPAP or bi-level pressure to deliver air without intubation, making them preferable for less severe cases and promoting comfort. Both types enhance the care and mobility of vulnerable newborns in various clinical settings.</p></p>
