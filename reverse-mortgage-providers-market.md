<p><p>Reverse mortgage providers operate mainly in three market types: Home Equity Conversion Mortgages (HECMs), which are federally insured loans allowing seniors to access home equity; Single-purpose reverse mortgages, often offered by state or local governments, designed for specific uses like home repairs or property taxes; and Proprietary reverse mortgages, which are private loans tailored for higher-value homes and can offer larger loan amounts. Each type serves different needs and financial situations for senior homeowners.</p></p>
