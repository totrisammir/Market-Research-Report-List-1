<p><p>The renal anemia treatment market primarily comprises iron supplements and erythropoiesis-stimulating agents (ESAs). </p><p>Iron supplements enhance iron levels, crucial for hemoglobin production, addressing iron deficiency commonly seen in chronic kidney disease (CKD) patients. </p><p>ESAs, on the other hand, stimulate the bone marrow to produce more red blood cells, countering anemia directly by addressing the underlying reduced erythropoietin production in CKD.
