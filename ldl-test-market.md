<p><p>The LDL test market encompasses various types of assessments for low-density lipoprotein, crucial for cardiovascular health. LDL-C measures cholesterol content in LDL particles, serving as a standard indicator of cardiovascular risk. LDL-P quantifies the number of LDL particles, providing insight into atherogenic risk. LDL-B assesses the size of LDL particles, with smaller particles being associated with higher cardiovascular risk. Additional tests, such as apoB, further enhance lipid profiling, catering to diverse diagnostic needs and improving personalized treatment approaches.</p></p>
