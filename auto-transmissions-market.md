<p><p>The auto transmission market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **Manual Transmission (MT)** - Requires driver engagement for gear shifts.</p><p>2. **Automatic Transmission (AT)** - Shifts gears automatically for ease of use.</p><p>3. **Automated Manual Transmission (AMT)** - Combines elements of manual and automatic, using sensors for gear changes.</p><p>4. **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)** - Offers seamless gear ratios for smoother acceleration.</p><p>5. **Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)** - Utilizes two clutches for quicker gear shifts, enhancing performance. </p><p>Each type serves specific driving preferences and performance needs.</p></p>
