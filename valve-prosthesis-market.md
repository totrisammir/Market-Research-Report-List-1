<p><strong>The Valve Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TranscatheterValve</li><li>Tissue Valve</li><li>Mechanical Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The valve prosthesis market is categorized into three main types: transcatheter valves, tissue valves, and mechanical valves. Transcatheter valves are minimally invasive and used in patients who are not suitable for open-heart surgery. Tissue valves, made from biological materials, mimic natural heart valves and generally have a limited lifespan. Mechanical valves, constructed from durable materials, offer longevity but require lifelong anticoagulation therapy. Each type caters to different patient needs and surgical risks, influencing treatment decisions in valve replacement procedures.</p></p>
