<p><p>The aircraft cargo handling equipment market encompasses several key types essential for efficient cargo operations. Container loaders facilitate the loading and unloading of cargo containers, ensuring swift access to the aircraft holds. Belt loaders transport baggage and cargo between the terminal and the aircraft. Cargo transporters move large amounts of cargo across airside operations, while cargo towing trucks assist in towing baggage carts and cargo dollies. Forklifts are utilized for lifting and moving heavy items within cargo handling areas.</p></p>
