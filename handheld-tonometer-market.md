<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Handheld Tonometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Applanation Tonometry</li><li>Indentation Tonometry</li><li>Rebound Tonometry</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld tonometer market features several measurement types: </p><p>1. **Applanation Tonometry**: Involves flattening the cornea to measure intraocular pressure. </p><p>2. **Indentation Tonometry**: Measures pressure by assessing how much the cornea indents under a weighted probe.</p><p>3. **Rebound Tonometry**: Uses a small probe that rebounds off the cornea to determine eye pressure, providing quick and contactless results.</p><p>4. **Others**: Include alternative technologies and methods that may not fit into the standard categories, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
