<p><p>The 3D CAD software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based CAD software enables users to access design tools and files from anywhere with an internet connection, promoting collaboration and scalability. In contrast, on-premise CAD solutions are installed directly on a user’s local hardware, offering greater control over data security and customization. Each type serves different business needs, with cloud solutions favoring flexibility and remote access, while on-premise options often prioritize data privacy and system integration.</p></p>
