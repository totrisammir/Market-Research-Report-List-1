<p><strong>The On-board Diagnostic (OBD) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threshold Monitoring</li><li>Comprehensive Component Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The On-board Diagnostic (OBD) system market is primarily divided into two types: Threshold Monitoring and Comprehensive Component Monitoring. Threshold Monitoring focuses on detecting malfunctions by comparing sensor readings against predefined limits, ensuring basic vehicle functionality and emission compliance. In contrast, Comprehensive Component Monitoring provides an in-depth analysis of individual components, tracking their performance and health to identify potential issues early. This broader approach enhances diagnostics, improves reliability, and contributes to better vehicle maintenance and efficiency.</p></p>
