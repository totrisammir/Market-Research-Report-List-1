<p><p>A strain gauge torque sensor is a device that measures torque by detecting the deformation (strain) of an object under load, using strain gauges bonded to its surface. When torque is applied, the strain gauges respond to the resulting deformation, allowing for precise torque measurement. These sensors are widely utilized in various applications, including automotive, aerospace, industrial machinery, and robotics, due to their accuracy and reliability.</p><p>The Strain Gauge Torque Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.60% during the forecast period.
