<p><strong>The Waveguide Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Branch Guide Coupler</li><li>Broadwall Coupler</li><li>Crossguide Coupler</li><li>Directional Coupler</li><li>Loop Coupler</li><li>Multihole Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waveguide couplers are essential components in guiding microwave signals, and their market comprises several types. Branch guide couplers split power into multiple paths, while broadwall couplers operate on the broad wall of waveguides for efficient coupling. Crossguide couplers utilize orthogonal waveguide axes. Directional couplers provide specific signal directions, enabling monitoring. Loop couplers enable non-intrusive signal sampling, and multihole couplers use multiple apertures for coupling, offering versatility in signal routing and measurement across various applications, such as telecommunications and radar systems.</p></p>
