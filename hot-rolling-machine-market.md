<p><ul><li>Rough Rolling Frame</li><li>Medium Rolling Rack</li><li>Finishing Rack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot rolling machine market is categorized into three main types: rough rolling frames, medium rolling racks, and finishing racks. Rough rolling frames are designed for initial shaping, converting large steel ingots into blooms or slabs. Medium rolling racks refine these shapes further, enhancing dimensions and mechanical properties. Finishing racks provide the final adjustments, ensuring precise thickness and surface quality. Together, these machines are essential in the production of steel and other metal products, catering to diverse industry requirements.</p></p>
