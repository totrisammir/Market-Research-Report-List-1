<p><p>The shielded isolation transformer market comprises two primary types: step-up and step-down transformers. Step-up transformers increase voltage levels, making them suitable for applications where higher voltage is required for efficient power transmission or to meet equipment specifications. Conversely, step-down transformers reduce voltage levels, ensuring safe and compatible power supply for devices operating at lower voltages. Both types enhance safety by isolating the output from the input, minimizing electrical noise and providing protection against voltage spikes.</p></p>
