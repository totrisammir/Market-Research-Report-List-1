<p><p>The Aviation Exhaust System market encompasses two primary types: Engine Exhaust Systems and APU (Auxiliary Power Unit) Exhaust Systems. Engine Exhaust Systems are crucial for managing the exhaust gases produced by aircraft engines, ensuring optimal performance and emissions control. In contrast, APU Exhaust Systems handle emissions from auxiliary power units, which provide electrical and pneumatic power while on the ground or during flight. Both systems are vital for enhancing aircraft efficiency, safety, and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
