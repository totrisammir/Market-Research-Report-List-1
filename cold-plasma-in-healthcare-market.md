<p><strong>The Cold Plasma in Healthcare Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-pressure Cold Plasma</li><li>Atmospheric Cold Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cold plasma in healthcare refers to ionized gas used for various medical applications, distinguished mainly by the pressure conditions in which it operates. Low-pressure cold plasma, generated in controlled vacuum environments, is effective for sterilization and wound healing due to its ability to penetrate surfaces efficiently. Atmospheric cold plasma, on the other hand, operates at ambient pressure and is suitable for surface decontamination and tissue treatment. Both types leverage unique properties to promote healing, enhance sterilization, and support regenerative medicine.</p></p>
