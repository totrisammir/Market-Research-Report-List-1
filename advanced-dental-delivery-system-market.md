<p><p>The Advanced Dental Delivery System market includes various types of delivery systems tailored for dental procedures. The Over-the-Patient System provides a streamlined approach by placing instruments directly overhead. Lastly, the Over-the-Head Delivery System suspends instruments for user convenience while maintaining patient comfort. Each system enhances efficiency and accessibility during dental treatments.</p></p>
