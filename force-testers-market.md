<p><p>Force testers are categorized into four main types: digital, mechanical, electric, and pneumatic. </p><p>Digital force testers utilize electronic sensors and provide precise measurements with digital displays, ideal for advanced testing. Mechanical testers rely on springs or weights, offering simplicity and reliability in basic applications. Electric force testers use motors for automated testing, providing speed and accuracy for high-volume tasks. Pneumatic force testers leverage compressed air for force application, suitable for dynamic testing scenarios where force needs to be consistently applied.</p></p>
