<p><ul><li>Pure Play Model</li><li>IDM Model</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS foundry service market comprises two primary models: the Pure Play Model and the IDM (Integrated Device Manufacturer) Model. The Pure Play Model involves foundries specializing exclusively in MEMS fabrication for multiple clients, offering flexibility and access to advanced technologies without competing products. In contrast, the IDM Model integrates design, fabrication, and packaging within a single entity, allowing for tighter control over production processes and proprietary technology but potentially limiting customer diversity. Each model serves unique business needs and market dynamics.</p></p>
