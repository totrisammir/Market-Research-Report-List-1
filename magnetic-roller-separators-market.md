<p><p>The Magnetic Roller Separators market is classified into two main types: Single Roller Magnetic Separators and Double Roller Magnetic Separators. Single Roller Magnetic Separators utilize one magnetic roller to efficiently separate ferrous and non-ferrous materials from bulk commodities. They are ideal for smaller-scale operations. In contrast, Double Roller Magnetic Separators feature two magnetic rollers, enhancing separation efficiency and capacity, making them suitable for larger industrial applications. Both types play a crucial role in improving material purity and recycling processes.</p></p>
