<p><strong>The Peripheral Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Balloon-expandable</li><li>Self-expandable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral stents market includes two primary types: balloon-expandable and self-expandable stents. Balloon-expandable stents are deployed using a balloon catheter that expands the stent at the site of the lesion, providing immediate support and offering a precise fit for smaller lesions. Conversely, self-expandable stents utilize a material that allows them to expand automatically upon release from the delivery system, adapting to the vessel's shape, making them ideal for more challenging or larger lesions. Both types enhance vessel patency and improve blood flow.</p></p>
